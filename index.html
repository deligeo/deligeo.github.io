My Journey in Tech

Hello! I’m Delna G, a passionate individual with a background in B Tech, now diving into the world of frontend development. My journey into tech began with a fascination for how things work behind the scenes, and it has evolved into a deep commitment to creating seamless and engaging user experiences.

I’m currently focused on honing my skills in Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy exploring frameworks like React. I believe that good design goes hand-in-hand with functionality, and I’m always looking for ways to enhance the usability and aesthetic of the projects I work on.

When I’m not coding, you can find me immersed in music. Whether I’m listening to my favourite tracks or singing along, music fuels my creativity and keeps me inspired. I love discovering new artists and genres, and I often draw parallels between the art of music and web design—both require creativity, emotion, and a strong sense of rhythm.

I also enjoy collaborating with others and sharing knowledge within the tech community. I believe that learning is a continuous journey, and I’m excited to connect with like-minded individuals who share my passion for innovation and creativity.

Thank you for visiting my site! I’m looking forward to the opportunities that lie ahead and am always open to new connections and projects. Let’s create something amazing together!
